Trans-Mississippi Civil War

Links related to pre-Civil War border conflicts and Civil War west of the Mississippi River

Much focus in mainstream history courses, biographies, histories on the Civil War have focused on activities east of the Allegany Mountains. Of the focus not in the East has been between the Mississippi River and the Allegany rivers. This segamented focus misses out on the bitter conflict west of the Mississippi River before the war officially began and during the war. Great bitterness existed amongst the population towards each other. Not grand armies but truly neighbors and family on other side.

This page provides links to information to Missouri\Kansas border conflict pre-war and battles that took place west of the Mississippi
